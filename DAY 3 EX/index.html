Vegetable Salad




Ingredients


1 cup of Mushrooms
1 cup of Tomatos, fresh or frozen
1 cup of Cucumbers
1 cup of Celery
1 cup of Garden beets
Green Leaf Lettuce
1/2 cup of yogurt (strawberry or vanilla)
1/2 cup of cool whip (or whip your own cream)



Steps To Make Vegetable Salad


  •     Place all of the prepared vegetables in a medium bowl

  •     Stir in Yogurt and Cool Whip.

  •     Refrigerate before serving.

  •     Sample the deliciousness… then gobble up a little more


Benifits of Vegetable Salad


Vegetables are a great source of vitamins and minerals. You won’t find a better nutritional source than veggies, which are packed with vitamins A, C and E, as well as magnesium, zinc, phosphorous and folic acid. For potassium, one of the most important minerals for your health, eat plenty of avocados, sweet potatoes, bananas, prunes and even tomato paste puree.
